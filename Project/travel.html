Travel






I love travelling as each travel is a new adventure, new story and new memory.

Also not a single travel can compare to each other, even if we visit the same destination over and over.

When I travel, I don't travel only to see mountains, lakes, beaches or monuments. I travel to experience different cultures, taste new cuisines, meet different people and challenge myself in an unknown city with an unfamiliar language. The unfamiliarity part excites me and I always look forward to a new adventure. I love meeting people from different parts of the world and listening to their stories.

You get to learn more about yourself, your emotions, your strengths and weaknesses. You face situations you had never before. The way you react in those situations teaches you something about yourself. It makes you more aware and stronger..

I have visited many countries and cities such as Venice, Milan, New York and Prague.
